Who we are

Helping Hearts is dedicated to creating a world in which anyone and everyone has access to high-quality, cost-effective mental health services.

Some of our other ongoing projects include:

Helping Hearts Buddy - an anonymous peer to peer support system that provides a safe space for people to open up about their issues to a volunteer
Volunteers on our Helping Heart Buddy platform undergo an online course that provides them with the basic training to be effective listeners.
Developing a chat-bot that would answer queries regarding mental health and well-being
